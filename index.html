What is ECHOS?

ECHOS or Electronic Communication Helper Online / Offline is an application software equipped with the latest generation of intelligence, allowing users to reach very advanced levels of learning in such a short time, and in very efficient ways. We offer detailed courses in many languages, as well as an open platform for chat between users to create relationships, and exchange in your chosen languages without forgetting the super intelligent A.I which answers and speaks to you in your preferred language.
